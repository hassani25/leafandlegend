The alliance means every formulation is monitored beyond the pharmacy counter — into the hands of patients, under the supervision of physicians and researchers who answer to an institutional review board, not a marketing department.

05 — The Origin
وليد حساني

Three Generations. One Conviction.

Walid Hassani was not searching for an industry. He was stewarding one. Born into the El Sghiar family of Ketama — three generations of Rif Valley cultivators — Hassani grew up inside the tradition that Morocco's Law 13-21 would eventually recognize and regulate.

When the law opened a path from ancestral cultivation to pharmaceutical formulation, Hassani did not see a business opportunity. He saw a responsibility — to prove that the Rif's botanical heritage could meet the highest standards of modern medicine without losing its identity, its integrity, or its chain of custody.

Leaf & Legend is that proof. Not a brand built on story, but a company built on traceability — from seed to pharmacy, from tradition to protocol, from conviction to observation.

"If you cannot trace your product from the field where it grew to the pharmacy where it is dispensed, you do not have a product. You have a story."
