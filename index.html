Heritage

Rooted in the Rif,
shaped by three generations

Leaf & Legend was born from the conviction that Morocco's cannabis heritage deserves a future beyond raw export. The founder's family has stewarded land in Ketama for three generations — not as a business asset, but as a living connection to the Rif Valley and its botanical traditions.

This is not a company that discovered cannabis through market research. This is a company that grew from the soil itself, and now seeks to transform what that soil produces: from unprocessed plant material to professionally formulated consumer products.
