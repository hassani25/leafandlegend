Not just hands.

The Method

Five steps.
Zero shortcuts.

Every product follows a disciplined sequence. No step can be skipped. No step can be reordered. The method is the guarantee that the transformation is real, not claimed.

01
Define the Plant
Identify the specific cultivar and cannabinoid profile rooted in the Rif Valley terroir. The altitude, the soil, the UV stress response. The plant definition sets the entire downstream chain in motion. Without a defined plant, there is no defined product.
Current Phase
02
Sign the MOU
Founder Mohamed El Sghiar formally signs the Memorandum of Understanding with the formulation partner. The MOU establishes formulation design authority, IP ownership (all filings at OMPIC under Leaf & Legend), exclusivity commitment, and compensation framework. This is the legal backbone of every product.
Completed
03
Receive the Preparation List
The formulation partner provides the preparation list: carrier system specifications, co-active ingredient selection, extraction parameters, potency targets, preservation protocols, and stability testing schedules. The list is the operational blueprint that translates formulation science into manufacturing steps.
In Progress
04
Find the Best Working Model
Evaluate which licensed laboratory partner can execute the formulation. Whether extraction parameters align with available equipment. Which carrier systems perform under Moroccan conditions. Whether potency targets are achievable with current biomass quality. Theory meets production capability.
Next
05
Organize the Work
Raw material sourcing. Extraction scheduling. Laboratory manufacturing slot booking. Quality control checkpoints. Regulatory filing preparation. Every person, every process, every deadline is assigned. Operational discipline is the difference between a good idea and a product in the market.
